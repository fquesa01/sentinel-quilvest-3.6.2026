. Bank Secrecy Act (BSA) / Anti-Money-Laundering (AML)
Overview

Primary Regulator: FinCEN, FDIC, OCC, Federal Reserve

Core Objective: Detect and prevent money laundering, terrorist financing, and suspicious transactions.

Applies To: Financial institutions, broker-dealers, fintechs, MSBs, certain attorneys and law firms handling client funds.

⚙️ Rule Templates
- code: STRUCTURED_CASH_TRANSACTIONS
  category: Money Laundering
  description: "Series of cash deposits just below $10k CTR threshold."
  trigger: "≥3 cash deposits between $9,000 and $9,999 within 7 days by same account."
  score: 75
  investigation: "Aggregate deposits, verify source of funds, interview client, file SAR if warranted."

- code: ROUND_NUMBER_WIRES
  category: Suspicious Wires
  description: "Outgoing wires in round amounts with no invoice or supporting documentation."
  trigger: "Wire amount % 1000 = 0 AND missing reference field."
  score: 60
  investigation: "Confirm business purpose; match to trade invoice; verify beneficiary ownership."

- code: SHELL_COMPANY_ACCOUNT
  category: Customer Risk
  description: "Customer entity with no physical operations, low employee count, and offshore registration."
  trigger: "Incorporation_country in ['BVI','Cayman','Panama'] AND employee_count < 3."
  score: 65
  investigation: "Perform Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD); collect beneficial ownership; screen for OFAC matches."

- code: PEP_ACCOUNT_ACTIVITY
  category: KYC / Sanctions
  description: "Politically Exposed Person (PEP) receives rapid high-value transfers."
  trigger: "PEP = true AND > $100,000 transferred within 7 days."
  score: 80
  investigation: "EDD + management approval for continued relationship; file SAR if unexplained."

- code: HIGH_RISK_JURISDICTION_WIRE
  category: Geographic Risk
  description: "Wire to FATF-listed jurisdiction or sanctioned country."
  trigger: "Destination_country in ['IR','SY','KP','CU','RU']."
  score: 85
  investigation: "Stop payment; sanctions screening; escalate to AML officer."

🇺🇸 2. USA PATRIOT Act (AML / Counter-Terrorist Financing)
Overview

Sections 311–326: Require Customer Identification Program (CIP) and enhanced due diligence.

Core Goal: Detect terrorist financing and prevent anonymous banking.

⚙️ Rule Templates
- code: MISSING_CUSTOMER_ID
  category: CIP Violation
  description: "Account opened without verified government ID."
  trigger: "CIP_verification_status = 'pending' AND account_opened > 5 days."
  score: 60
  investigation: "Suspend transactions; contact client for KYC completion."

- code: OFAC_UNSCREENED_NEW_ACCOUNT
  category: Sanctions Risk
  description: "New customer not screened against OFAC lists."
  trigger: "account_created = true AND screening_timestamp = null."
  score: 70
  investigation: "Immediate OFAC screen; freeze account pending clearance."

- code: HIGH_RISK_CASH_INTENSIVE_BUSINESS
  category: Risk Profiling
  description: "Business type flagged high risk (nightclub, casino, car dealer)."
  trigger: "NAICS_code in ['722410','713210','441110']."
  score: 55
  investigation: "EDD required; document site inspection and ownership verification."
