🧩 1. Price-Fixing & Bid-Rigging
- code: PRICE_FIX_COMMS
  category: Collusion
  description: "Communications coordinating prices, discounts, surcharges, or bid terms."
  trigger: "Email/chat with competitor domain + keywords ['price', 'increase', 'hold firm', 'match rates']"
  examples: ["Let's align our Q2 price increases", "We’ll both keep margins at 10%"]
  score: 70
  investigation: "Preserve messages; notify antitrust counsel; compare historical pricing patterns."

- code: BID_ROTATION
  category: Procurement
  description: "Competitors winning bids in repeating rotation pattern."
  trigger: "≥3 bids alternating winners among ≤5 bidders over 12 months."
  score: 75
  investigation: "Analyze bid data; confirm independent pricing; escalate to legal."

- code: IDENTICAL_BIDS
  category: Procurement
  description: "Two or more bidders submit identical bids with same errors or rounding."
  trigger: "Exact match in bid amount, format, and timestamp ±5 min."
  score: 65
  investigation: "Check metadata & source IP; audit procurement integrity."

- code: TERRITORY_SPLIT
  category: Market Division
  description: "Agreements dividing markets, regions, or clients."

  score: 70

- code: CUSTOMER_NON_SOLICIT

  score: 55

🏢 3. Monopolization & Abuse of Dominance
- code: BELOW_COST_PRICING
  category: Dominance
  description: "Sustained pricing below average variable cost with intent to exclude rivals."
  trigger: "Gross margin <0% for ≥2 quarters + competitor exits."
  score: 50
  investigation: "Economic analysis; document cost justification."

- code: EXCLUSIVE_DEAL
  category: Dominance
  description: "Contract grants exclusivity >1 year or rebates ≥15% for share loyalty."
  trigger: "Exclusive terms in >25% of total sales volume."
  score: 45
  investigation: "Legal review; ensure efficiencies outweigh foreclosure."

- code: TYING_ARRANGEMENT
  category: Dominance
  description: "Product A sold only if customer also buys Product B."
  trigger: "Sales system enforces mandatory bundle; no standalone SKU."
  score: 40
  investigation: "Assess product integration rationale; consult counsel."

- code: COMPETITOR_MERGER
