USA PATRIOT Act / KYC & Sanctions Compliance Rule Library v1.0
Aspect	Coverage
Regulators	FinCEN • OCC • FDIC • SEC • FINRA • Federal Reserve
Core Sections	Title III (International Money Laundering Abatement and Financial Anti-Terrorism Act of 2001) §§ 311–326
Purpose	Identify customers (KYC), detect terrorist financing, block transactions with prohibited parties, and maintain reporting records.
Applies To	Banks, fintech firms, broker-dealers, MSBs, attorneys handling funds, and any entity engaged in cross-border transactions.

⚙️ 1 | Customer Identification Program (CIP)
- code: CIP_MISSING_ID
  category: KYC / CIP
  description: "Account opened without verified government ID or acceptable documentation."
  trigger: "customer.verified_id=false OR id_expired=true"
  score: 75
  examples: ["Client submitted account form with photocopy of expired passport."]
  investigation: "Suspend account; obtain valid ID; log CIP exception."

- code: CIP_DUPLICATE_ID
  category: KYC / CIP
  description: "Duplicate identity documents used across multiple customers."
  trigger: "id_number used_by_count > 1"
  score: 65
  investigation: "Check for identity theft; escalate to fraud investigation; file SAR if needed."

- code: INCOMPLETE_BENEFICIAL_OWNER_INFO
  category: KYC / BOI
  description: "Missing ownership data for legal-entity customer."
  trigger: "beneficial_owner_count < required_threshold"
  score: 70
  investigation: "Collect BOI form (FinCEN BOI CIP); verify control person; update file."
