Considerations / Things to Evaluate

Since you are building or evaluating workflows (and given your background in technology/data), here are some considerations:

Flexibility vs complexity: As noted by users, while Relativity offers great customization and extensibility, the trade-off can be a steeper learning curve and more configuration overhead. 
Reddit
+1

Cloud vs on-prem: RelativityOne is the cloud SaaS offering; check data-residency, security/regulation compliance (especially if cross-border).

Licensing / cost: Advanced modules (aiR, Contracts, etc) may be additional cost.

Integration and APIs: If you plan to build connectors (for example, your LifeChain product linking to medical records), check the API/SDK, scripting capabilities, automation support.

Review workflow efficiency: Even with all the features, the way you set up templates/workflows, train reviewers, apply analytics will affect cost/time savings.

Data reduction and defensibility: Features like de-duplication, email threading, clustering reduce volume, but you’ll want to ensure audit trails, defensible workflows are in place.
