Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) / DFARS Compliance Library v1.0
Aspect	Coverage
Regulators	DoD DCMA • GSA • NASA • DOJ Inspector General • SIGAR
Governing Parts	FAR Subparts 3, 9, 15, 19, 31, 42, 52 • DFARS 203, 204, 209, 252
Purpose	Ensure procurement integrity, truthful cost/pricing data, ethics in contracting, secure information handling, and lawful subcontractor conduct.
Applies To	Prime & sub-contractors, federal suppliers, consultants, grantees.

⚙️ 1. Procurement Integrity (FAR 3.104)
- code: BID_LEAK
  category: Procurement Integrity
  description: "Evidence of bid or source-selection information shared prior to award."
  trigger: "Email or meeting log references competitor pricing or 'source-selection' before RFP close."
  score: 85
  examples: ["Got intel on ABC’s bid numbers before submission."]
  investigation: "Freeze contract file; interview staff; notify contracting officer & legal; potential 18 U.S.C §1905 violation."

- code: IMPROPER_CONTACT
  category: Procurement Integrity
  description: "Unauthorized communication between government official and contractor during blackout period."
  trigger: "Contact_date between solicitation release and award AND no CO approval record."
  score: 70
  investigation: "Document contact; report to CO; issue ethics retraining."

- code: POST_EMPLOYMENT_RECRUIT
  category: Revolving Door / Ethics
  description: "Offer of employment to government employee involved in procurement decision."
  trigger: "Candidate.former_agency_role='contracting' AND related_award within 1 year."
  score: 75
  investigation: "Suspend hiring; legal review for 41 U.S.C §2104 breach."

💰 2. Truthful Cost & Pricing (FAR 15.403-4 / FAR 52.215-10 TINA)
- code: DEFECTIVE_PRICING
  category: Cost & Pricing
  description: "Certified cost or pricing data inaccurate, incomplete, or non-current at award."
  trigger: "Audit variance > 10 % between proposal and actual cost data."
  score: 80
  investigation: "Perform sweep; calculate price reduction; issue disclosure to CO under TINA."

- code: UNALLOWABLE_COST
  category: Cost Principles
  description: "Costs claimed that are expressly unallowable under FAR 31 ( e.g., alcohol, fines )."
  trigger: "GL account flagged 'Unallowable' AND included in claim."
  score: 65
  investigation: "Adjust indirect rates; refund; update chart-of-accounts tagging."

- code: COST_MISALLOCATION
  category: Indirect Cost Rates
  description: "Improper allocation of overhead or G&A to government contract."
  trigger: "Pool variance > 15 % or expense charged to contract without allocation base."
  score: 60
  investigation: "Recalculate indirects; update CASB Disclosure Statement; resubmit invoices."

🧮 3. Billing & Payment Integrity (FAR Subpart 32)
- code: PROGRESS_BILLING_NO_SUPPORT
  category: Billing
  description: "Progress payment submitted without supporting cost records or CO approval."
  trigger: "Invoice.type='progress' AND attachments missing."
  score: 55
  investigation: "Request substantiation; delay payment until validated."

- code: DUPLICATE_INVOICE
  category: Billing
  description: "Same invoice number submitted twice within 90 days."
  trigger: "invoice_number duplicate AND amount identical."
  score: 50
  investigation: "Reject duplicate; audit internal controls."

- code: ADVANCE_PAYMENT_WITHOUT_AUTH
  category: Billing
  description: "Advance payment issued without contractual authorization per FAR 32.4."
  trigger: "Payment_type='advance' AND clause 52.232-12 absent."
  score: 65
  investigation: "Escalate to Finance; recover funds; update terms."

🧰 5. Subcontractor Oversight (FAR 52.244-2 / DFARS 252.244-7001)
- code: UNAPPROVED_SUBCONTRACT
  category: Supply Chain
  description: "Subcontract issued without CO or DCMA approval where required."
  trigger: "Sub_value > threshold AND approval_status ≠ approved."
  score: 70
  investigation: "Suspend payments; seek retro approval; document deviation."

- code: FLOWDOWN_CLAUSE_OMITTED
  category: Flowdowns
  description: "Mandatory FAR/DFARS clauses missing in subcontract template."
  trigger: "Template missing required clauses e.g., 52.219-8, 252.204-7012."
  score: 60
  investigation: "Amend subcontract; notify subcontractor; update contract library."

- code: SMALL_BUSINESS_MISREP
  category: Socio-Economic Programs
  description: "Subcontractor self-certified as small/disadvantaged without SBA record."
  trigger: "SAM.gov status ≠ small AND claim =true."
  score: 65
  investigation: "Report false certification to SBA/OIG; adjust credit claims."

🔐 6. Cybersecurity & Data Safeguards (DFARS 252.204-7012 / FAR 52.204-21)
- code: CUI_DATA_BREACH
  category: Cybersecurity
  description: "Controlled Unclassified Information (CUI) exfiltration or unauthorized access."
  trigger: "Security incident = CUI AND notification_time > 72 h."
  score: 85
  investigation: "Report to DIBNet within 72 h; isolate systems; forensic review."

- code: NIST_SP800_171_NONCOMPLIANT
  category: Cybersecurity
  description: "Supplier lacks implemented security controls per NIST SP 800-171."
  trigger: "Controls_implemented < 110 AND plan_of_action missing."
  score: 70
  investigation: "Suspend CUI access; require POA&M; update SPRS score."

- code: EXPORT_CONTROLLED_TECH_TRANSFER
  category: Data Security
  description: "ITAR/EAR data transferred to non-U.S. citizen without TAA."
  trigger: "file.tag='ITAR' AND user.citizenship ≠ US."
  score: 80
  investigation: "Stop access; file voluntary disclosure to DDTC."

🧑‍💼 7. Ethics & Business Conduct (FAR 52.203-13)
- code: GIFTS_TO_OFFICIALS
  category: Ethics
  description: "Employee gave gift > $20 to federal official."
  trigger: "Gift.amount > 20 AND recipient.role='federal employee'."
  score: 65
  investigation: "Recover gift; record violation; disciplinary review."

- code: KICKBACK_SCHEME
  category: Ethics
  description: "Kickbacks from subcontractors to procurement staff."
  trigger: "Payment from vendor to employee personal account within 30 days of award."
  score: 80
  investigation: "Forensic audit; terminate relationship; report to OIG."

- code: HOTLINE_REPORT_IGNORED
  category: Ethics
  description: "Internal hotline report not investigated within 30 days."
  trigger: "Case.status='open' AND age > 30."
  score: 55
  investigation: "Assign investigator; document findings; close within 60 days."

💬 11. Explain-This-Flag Prompt Template
prompt_template: |
  You are a federal-contracting compliance analyst.
  Explain why this transaction or behavior triggered a FAR/DFARS red flag.
  Include:
  1. Summary of facts (contract, vendor, amount, date).
  2. Applicable FAR/DFARS clause and regulatory section.
  3. Risk typology (procurement integrity, cost mischarge, ethics, cybersecurity, etc.).
  4. Potential penalties (debarment, termination for cause, FCA liability, civil penalty).
  5. Recommended next steps:
     - Preserve contract and email records  
     - Notify Contracting Officer or OIG  
     - Suspend payment or performance  
     - Implement corrective action plan
  Keep tone objective, fact-based, and audit-ready.
