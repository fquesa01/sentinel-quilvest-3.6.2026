🌍 Office of Foreign Assets Control (OFAC) Sanctions Compliance Rule Library v1.0
Aspect	Coverage
Regulator	U.S. Department of the Treasury – OFAC
Legal Authority	International Emergency Economic Powers Act (IEEPA), Trading with the Enemy Act (TWEA), Executive Orders
Program Scope	Sanctions against individuals, entities, sectors, and countries (SDN, SSI, NS-ISA, etc.)
Purpose	Prevent U.S. persons and companies from transacting with prohibited parties, assets, or jurisdictions.
Applies To	Financial institutions, exporters/importers, software and tech companies, legal and professional services firms, any entity handling cross-border funds or trade.

⚙️ 1 | Sanctions Screening (SDN, SSI, Consolidated Lists)
- code: SDN_NAME_MATCH
  category: Screening
  description: "Customer or counterparty name matches OFAC SDN list ≥ 90%."
  trigger: "fuzzy_name_score >= 0.9"
  score: 85
  examples: ["Payment to 'Rosneft Trading SA' matches OFAC SDN entry."]
  investigation: "Stop transaction; freeze funds; verify match via secondary fields (DOB, address); file blocked property report within 10 days."

- code: SSI_ENTITY_MATCH
  category: Sectoral Sanctions
  description: "Entity on SSI list involved in prohibited equity or debt transaction."
  trigger: "entity.SSI_listed = true AND security_type in ['equity','debt'] AND maturity > 14 days"
  score: 75
  investigation: "Block prohibited transaction; consult legal for General License coverage."

- code: CONSOLIDATED_LIST_HIT
  category: Screening
  description: "Match against non-SDN consolidated lists (UN, EU, BIS, State)."
  trigger: "list_type='non-SDN' AND match_score >= 0.85"
  score: 70
  investigation: "Perform Enhanced Due Diligence (EDD); escalate to sanctions team for clearance."

💳 2 | Payment & Funds Transfer Monitoring
- code: SANCTIONED_COUNTRY_PAYMENT
  category: Transaction Risk
  description: "Payment to or from jurisdiction subject to OFAC country-wide sanctions."
  trigger: "sender_country in ['IR','SY','KP','CU'] OR receiver_country in ['IR','SY','KP','CU']"
  score: 90
  investigation: "Block transfer; notify OFAC; file blocking report; assess for General License."

- code: THIRD_PARTY_INTERMEDIARY
  category: Routing Risk
  description: "Transaction routed through bank located in sanctioned country or owned by SDN."
  trigger: "intermediary_bank.country_code in ['RU','BY','IR','SY'] OR intermediary_bank.owner.SDN=true"
  score: 80
  investigation: "Review SWIFT chain; reroute via compliant correspondent; file report."

- code: ROUND_TRIP_PAYMENT
  category: Trade-Based Laundering
  description: "Funds leave and return through different counterparties in high-risk regions."
  trigger: "inflow_country = outflow_country AND counterparties differ AND period ≤ 7 days"
  score: 65
  investigation: "Analyze purpose; trace beneficial owners; possible evasion scheme."

🚢 3 | Trade & Export Transactions
- code: BLOCKED_PORT_OF_LADING
  category: Shipping
  description: "Export or import shipment involves sanctioned port or vessel."
  trigger: "port.country_code in ['RU','IR','SY','KP','CU'] OR vessel.IMO in SDN_vessels"
  score: 85
  investigation: "Hold shipment; file BIS and OFAC voluntary disclosure."

- code: DUAL_USE_EXPORT
  category: Export Control
  description: "Export license required for item with ECCN flagged 'dual-use' to sanctioned region."
  trigger: "ECCN in control_list AND dest_country in embargoed_country_list"
  score: 75
  investigation: "Block export; coordinate with BIS/OFAC compliance counsel."

- code: FALSE_END_USER_STATEMENT
  category: Export Documentation
  description: "Mismatch between declared and verified end-user or destination."
  trigger: "end_user_country ≠ shipping_country OR end_user_verified=false"
  score: 70
  investigation: "Confirm ultimate consignee; potential sanctions evasion; alert legal."

🧾 4 | Beneficial Ownership & Indirect Control (50 Percent Rule)
- code: INDIRECT_SDN_OWNERSHIP
  category: Beneficial Ownership
  description: "Entity 50% or more owned (individually or collectively) by SDN entities."
  trigger: "sum(owners.where(SDN=true).ownership_pct) ≥ 50"
  score: 85
  investigation: "Block entity; document ownership chain; file report."

- code: UNVERIFIED_BENEFICIAL_OWNER
  category: Ownership Risk
  description: "Customer or vendor ownership structure incomplete or unverifiable."
  trigger: "ownership_verified=false OR beneficial_owners < required_threshold"
  score: 60
  investigation: "Collect ownership certificates; confirm with registries; freeze until verified."

  category: Structuring
  description: "Multiple small transactions below sanctions screening threshold to same party."
  trigger: "count(transactions_to_party < $5k within 24h) ≥ 3"
  score: 65
  investigation: "Aggregate flows; analyze evasion intent; consider SAR filing."

- code: ALTERNATE_SPELLING_EVASION

  trigger: "fuzzy_name_score 0.8-0.89 AND country high_risk"
  score: 60
