> * **DSL primitives:** `TEXT.CONTAINS_ANY`, `TEXT.NEAR(terms, window)`, `ATTACHMENT.FILETYPE`, `ATTACHMENT.FILENAME.MATCHES`, `CURRENCY.MENTIONED`, `ENTITY.{...}`, `COUNTRY.IN(list)`, `METADATA.SENT_AT.WITHIN_DAYS_OF(date, n)`, `COUNTERSIGNALS.NOT([...])`.

> * **Scoring:** Defaults map to your **20–90 risk engine**; calibrate with evals.

# FCPA — 10 starter rules

### **FCPA‑01 — Facilitation / “Grease” Payment Language**

**Intent:** Surface explicit/euphemistic bribe requests around officials.
**Trigger (DSL):**

```
RULE "FCPA - Facilitation / Grease"
WHEN channel IN ["email","slack","teams"]
 AND TEXT.CONTAINS_ANY($BRIBE_EUPHEMISMS)
 AND TEXT.NEAR($GOV_OFFICIAL_TERMS, 12)
 AND (CURRENCY.MENTIONED OR ATTACHMENT.FILETYPE IN ["xls","pdf","docx"])
 AND COUNTERSIGNALS.NOT($COUNTERSIGNALS)
THEN ALERT severity=HIGH risk=85 tags=["FCPA","Anti-Bribery"] route="Attorney Review"
```

**Notes:** Add local euphemisms per region; keep a living dictionary.
