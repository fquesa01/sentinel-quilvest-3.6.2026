Gramm-Leach-Bliley Act (GLBA) Compliance Rule Library v1.0
Aspect	Coverage
Regulators	FTC, CFPB, OCC, FDIC, NCUA, Federal Reserve
Governing Parts	15 U.S.C. §§ 6801–6809; 16 CFR Parts 313 & 314
Purpose	Protect the confidentiality and integrity of consumer financial information, require safeguards and privacy disclosures, and prevent unauthorized access (“pretexting”).
Applies To	Financial institutions, lenders, mortgage servicers, fintechs, insurance and investment advisers, and any entity that collects non-public personal information (NPI).

⚙️ 1 | Privacy Rule (16 CFR Part 313)
- code: MISSING_PRIVACY_NOTICE
  category: Privacy Rule
  description: "No initial or annual privacy notice provided to customer."
  trigger: "customer.created = true AND (initial_notice_sent = false OR annual_notice_sent=false)"
  score: 70
  examples: ["Account opened but no GLBA privacy notice delivered."]
  investigation: "Send compliant notice immediately; document late delivery; update CRM workflow."

- code: INCOMPLETE_DISCLOSURE
  category: Privacy Rule
  description: "Privacy notice omits required elements (categories of NPI shared, affiliates, opt-out rights)."
  trigger: "notice_template missing ['categories_shared','opt_out_method','policy_changes']"
  score: 60
  investigation: "Revise notice; confirm distribution; legal review before reissue."

- code: OPT_OUT_NOT_PROCESSED
  category: Privacy Rule
  description: "Customer opt-out request not implemented within 30 days."
  trigger: "optout_request_date + 30 < current_date AND share_status='active'"
  score: 75
  investigation: "Cease sharing immediately; confirm data removal; report control failure to Privacy Officer."

🛡️ 2 | Safeguards Rule (16 CFR Part 314)
- code: NO_RISK_ASSESSMENT
  category: Safeguards
  description: "Required risk assessment missing or older than 12 months."
  trigger: "last_risk_assessment_date > 365 days OR null"
  score: 55
  investigation: "Schedule assessment; update safeguards plan; retain signed report."

- code: UNENCRYPTED_CUSTOMER_DATA
  category: Safeguards
  description: "Sensitive NPI stored or transmitted without encryption."
  trigger: "storage.encryption=false OR transmission.TLS=false"
  score: 80
  investigation: "Encrypt files; validate key-management logs; disciplinary review if negligent."

- code: ACCESS_CONTROL_FAILURE
  category: Safeguards
  description: "Shared credentials or stale user access to customer data."
  trigger: "user.last_login > 90 days AND access_revoked=false"
  score: 70
  investigation: "Revoke inactive accounts; run entitlement review; implement MFA."

- code: THIRD_PARTY_SECURITY_GAP
  category: Vendor Risk
  description: "Vendor with NPI access lacks security questionnaire or due-diligence review."
  trigger: "vendor.access_scope includes 'NPI' AND vendor.assessment_date > 12 months"
  score: 65
  investigation: "Suspend data exchange; obtain SOC 2/ISO cert; update vendor-risk register."

- code: INCIDENT_RESPONSE_GAP
  category: Safeguards
  description: "No incident-response test or tabletop exercise in last 12 months."
  trigger: "last_IR_test_date > 365 days OR null"
  score: 45
  investigation: "Schedule IR test; record lessons learned; integrate into training."

  score: 80
  investigation: "Block request; alert security; log in threat register; notify customer if breach."

- code: INTERNAL_DATA_SNOOPING
