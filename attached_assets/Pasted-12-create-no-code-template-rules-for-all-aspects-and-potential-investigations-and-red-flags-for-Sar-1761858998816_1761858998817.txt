🧩 1. Financial Reporting Integrity (Section 302 / 404)
- code: MANUAL_JOURNAL_OVERRIDE
  category: Financial Reporting
  description: "Manual journal entries posted without independent review or approval."
  trigger: "Journal entry manually created >$50,000 by preparer without secondary approver."
  examples: ["End-of-quarter revenue adjustment by same user who approved it."]
  score: 60
  investigation: "Review justification, supporting docs, and audit trail; verify management sign-off."

- code: LATE_ENTRY_POSTING
  category: Financial Reporting
  description: "Entries posted after period close without controller approval."
  trigger: "Posting timestamp > period close date and no documented approval."
  score: 45
  investigation: "Validate late entry authorization; ensure disclosure in audit memo."

- code: REVERSAL_PATTERN
  category: Financial Reporting
  description: "Large entry reversed within 30 days with no explanatory memo."
  trigger: "Debit > $25 000 reversed < 30 days later."
  score: 35
  investigation: "Check rationale; ensure not used to shift income between periods."

🏦 2. Segregation of Duties (SOD) Controls
- code: SOD_CONFLICT_USER
  category: Access Control
  description: "User holds conflicting roles: transaction creator + approver or vendor master + payment release."
  trigger: "Role matrix conflict between Finance and Procurement modules."
  score: 70
  investigation: "Deactivate access; review transactions for self-approval or fraud."

- code: MULTI_ROLE_ACCESS
  category: Access Control
  description: "User has production access in both accounting and IT systems."
  trigger: "Same account in ERP and database admin group."
  score: 50
  investigation: "Review logs for unauthorized changes; apply least-privilege principle."

- code: TEMP_ROLE_ESCALATION
  category: Access Control
  description: "Temporary admin access not revoked within allowed window."
  trigger: "Access granted for 24 hrs; still active after 72 hrs."
  score: 40
  investigation: "Disable access; document root cause; update IAM process."

  description: "Production code/config changes without ticket or CAB approval."
  trigger: "Commit or deployment in production branch lacking linked change ticket."
  score: 55
  investigation: "Identify responsible engineer; verify emergency-change documentation."

- code: LOG_DELETION
  category: ITGC

  score: 65
  investigation: "Preserve remaining logs; determine intent; escalate to CISO and audit."

- code: FAILED_BACKUP
  category: ITGC
  description: "Critical financial database backup failed or not verified."
  trigger: "Backup job failed > 2 days in a row."
  score: 45
  investigation: "Initiate backup restoration test; document remediation."
